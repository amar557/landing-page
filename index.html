behind the scenes

At A Dog Person, we believe our furry friends deserve nothing but the best.

Our journey began with two dedicated dog parents, Emily and Mukasa, in London. Their beloved dog Teddy, who you'll see featured throughout our story, inspired them to create something special after experiencing common health issues like skin problems, joint pain, and poor nutrition. When Teddy crossed the rainbow bridge, Emily and Mukasa became determined to develop products that genuinely improve the health and happiness of dogs everywhere.

The pet industry is notorious for prioritising profits over pets, often using low-quality ingredients and fillers.

At A Dog Person, we stand against this trend. Our mission is to offer supplements made from transparent, high-quality ingredients that truly make a difference. We believe in being open about what goes into our products because we know that as a true dog person, you want nothing but the best for your fur babies.

Our commitment to transparency, effectiveness, and the well-being of our pets is what sets us apart.

We are dedicated to ensuring that every product we create supports a healthier, happier, and longer life for dogs. Because at A Dog Person, we’re not just about products—we’re about doing right by our dogs, every step of the way.
